# Final Response Source Notes

Status: working source notes  
Date: 2026-06-30

Use these source groups in the final response.

## Customer Proof / Enterprise Data

- Databricks customers: https://www.databricks.com/customers  
  Use to support the pattern of customer proof, industry grouping, and customer stories.

- Snowflake customers: https://www.snowflake.com/en/customers/  
  Use to support customer proof, story cards, and outcome-rich proof patterns that GTG should not imitate without approved case copy.

- Confluent customers: https://www.confluent.io/customers/  
  Use to support filterable customer proof structures by industry, region, product, or topic.

## Brand / Design Systems

- IBM Design Language: https://www.ibm.com/design/language/  
  Use to support the idea that a mature technology brand is a system of logo, color, icon, illustration, data visualization, diagrams, motion, and resources.

- Carbon Design System: https://carbondesignsystem.com/  
  Use to support reusable primitives and systematic technical design.

- Frontify visual identity guide: https://www.frontify.com/en/guide/visual-identity  
  Use to support visual identity documentation and reusable brand asset rules.

- McGill visual identity guide: https://www.mcgill.ca/visual-identity/visual-identity-guide  
  Use to support the idea that logos, colors, typography, templates, photography, graphics, and layouts belong to a full identity system.

## Motion / Accessibility

- Carbon Motion: https://carbondesignsystem.com/elements/motion/overview/  
  Use to support purposeful, consistent, adaptive motion and duration discipline.

- Atlassian Motion: https://atlassian.design/foundations/motion/  
  Use to support motion as interaction clarity and brand expression, not decoration.

- W3C WAI images tutorial: https://www.w3.org/WAI/tutorials/images/  
  Use to support decorative vs informative image treatment.

- W3C WAI complex images: https://www.w3.org/WAI/tutorials/images/complex/  
  Use to support text alternatives for charts, flow charts, and diagrams.

- Section508.gov alternative text: https://www.section508.gov/create/alternative-text/  
  Use to support meaningful alternatives for informative images.

- Data.europa.eu accessible SVG and ARIA: https://data.europa.eu/apps/data-visualisation-guide/accessible-svg-and-aria  
  Use to support accessible inline SVG patterns.
